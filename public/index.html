Oliver Studio

Residential & Hospitality Interiors

We approach interiors through the lens of storytelling — layering art, furniture, lighting, and architecture to create spaces with mood, character, and depth.

Oliver Studio is a Los Angeles–based interior studio specializing in residential and hospitality interiors. Founded by television showrunner and writer Tracy Oliver, the studio brings a cinematic approach to space — blending sculptural furniture, contemporary art, and thoughtful lighting to create environments that feel layered, expressive, and lived in.
