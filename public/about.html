About the Studio

Oliver Studio is a Los Angeles–based interior studio focused on residential and hospitality interiors.

Founded by television showrunner and writer Tracy Oliver, the studio approaches interiors through the lens of storytelling. Drawing on a background in visual narrative and set design, each space is treated as a composition — where art, furniture, lighting, and architecture work together to create mood and character.

The studio’s work blends sculptural furniture, contemporary art, and warm modern materials to create spaces that feel layered, expressive, and deeply livable.
